Comparison: Latitude vs. ________

The goal of this project was to analyze various weather patterns in relation to latitude. The original analysis was done using linear regression and matplotlib in Jupyter Notebooks to analyze data of 500+ cities from the OpenWeather API. The following relationships were analyzed and are available to the right and in the dropdown menu:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
