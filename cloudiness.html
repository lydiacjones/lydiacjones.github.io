Comparison: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Analysis:

When comparing these two attributes, there really does not appear to be a correlation between latitude and cloudiness. What you do notice is how weathermen tend to round to the nearest 10 or 5%, giving the appearance of clusters around 0%, 20%, 40%, 75%, and 100% cloudiness. That feature in the data prevents it from being easily analyzed - there is likely a lot of error when the values are rounded to the nearest 5 or 10%.
